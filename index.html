Story

From childhood laughter to teenage adventures, every moment has led me to this special day. A day to celebrate growth, friendship, and all the memories that made me who I am today. I am thrilled to invite you to join me in celebrating my debut a night full of joy, love, and unforgettable moments. Let’s make this milestone a beautiful memory together!

Who is Francine?

Hello! My name is Francine, though some call me Chinny or Nicole. I was born on December 18, 2007. Ever since I was young, I’ve had a passion for dancing. It’s more than just a hobby, it’s a way for me to express my emotions, have fun, and challenge myself. Through it all, dancing has brought me confidence, joy, and a love for self-expression.
